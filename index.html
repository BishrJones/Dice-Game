Let's Play Dice!

CHOOSE YOUR CHIPS.

1 chip = $25

Balance: $0

1) Choose an amount of chips to start the game with.
2) Once you have chosen your chips, in the input field put the amount you would like to wager with.
3) Since this is your first roll, click the first roll button to get your point (7 or 11 wins on first roll).
4) If the initial roll grants you a number of anything other than 7 or 11, you will be prompted to click the point roll button to continue rolling until you either roll your point(win) or crap out(lose).
